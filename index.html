Built with your privacy & experience in mind

Modern technology that respects your data and works everywhere

Progressive Web App (PWA)

Install directly from your browser—no app store needed. Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop. Updates automatically, saves space, and works offline.

Privacy-First Architecture

Your data stays yours, always. No tracking, no ads, no selling your information. We don't use your data for anything except providing you the service.

No Bank Linking Required

We deliberately don't connect to your bank. This means enhanced security, no third-party access to your accounts, and you control what gets tracked.

Offline-First Design

Track spending anywhere, even without internet. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online. Perfect for travel or spotty connections.

Lightning Fast Performance

Optimized for speed and responsiveness. No lag, no waiting. Add a transaction in under 3 seconds, see insights instantly.

Secure by Design

Your data is securely hosted on Google Firebase with industry-leading end-to-end encryption. Your financial data is protected at every layer.

Always Up-to-Date

Automatic updates mean you always have the latest features and security improvements without visiting an app store.

Open & Transparent

Code is publicly available. No hidden algorithms, no black boxes. You can verify exactly what we're doing with your data (nothing!).
